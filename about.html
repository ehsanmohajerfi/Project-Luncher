I’m Ehsan Mohajer, a full-stack software developer in Jyväskylä, Finland. I help people turn ideas and business needs into clear, reliable digital products.

From learning to building

My journey through Jyväskylä University of Applied Sciences strengthened the connection between practical problem-solving and software development. It taught me to look beyond individual features and understand the people, processes and goals around a digital product.

My previous experience as a Project Specialist added another perspective: successful delivery depends on communication, organisation, documentation and the ability to connect technical work with real operational needs.

Today, through Sani Studio, I design and develop business websites, full-stack applications, AI-assisted tools and automation. Through Sani Portfolio, I share my professional history and selected work. Sani Portal brings those paths together.

I don’t promise that technology alone will make every dream real. I offer something more practical: careful listening, honest guidance and the engineering work needed to turn a strong idea into a useful product.

QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Frequently asked questions

How does your field of study influence your work?

My software-development studies developed both technical thinking and structured problem-solving. I approach projects by understanding requirements, modelling the solution, building incrementally and validating the result with real users and business goals.

What is full-stack development?

Full-stack development covers both the customer-facing interface and the systems behind it—such as application logic, databases, authentication and integrations. It allows me to understand how the complete product works rather than treating each layer separately.

How does your Project Specialist experience help clients?

It strengthens planning, communication, documentation and stakeholder awareness. These skills are especially useful when a project involves several systems, changing requirements or people with different technical backgrounds.

Can you help before development begins?

Yes. Planning is often the most valuable first step. I can help clarify the audience, business outcome, scope, user journey and technical options before implementation starts.

Do you only work with customers in Finland?

No. I am based in Jyväskylä and can work with Finnish customers locally, but remote collaboration is available worldwide in English, Finnish and Persian.

Where should I go next?

Visit Sani Studio when you want to start a website or digital-product project. Visit Sani Portfolio for my résumé, previous experience and detailed project history. Use the Sani Portal contact page when you are unsure which path fits your need.
